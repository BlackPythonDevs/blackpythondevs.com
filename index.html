What is Black Python Devs

Black Python Devs is an online-first community for python developers of all skill levels that identify as Black.

Our goal is to:

  1. Create a sense of community amongst Black Developers globally.
    2. community.
  2. Support Black Python developers with career development resources.
  3. Share the experiences of other groups with communities of Black developers.
